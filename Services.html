Tok Tokkie Shuttles Namibia offers weekly schedule all the way to Gaborone, Botswana and from Windhoek

Bus Scheduled Shuttle Services between:

  • Windhoek to Hosea Kutako International Airport and Returns

    We have scheduled shared departures between Windhoek from Christ Church near Parliament and Hosea Kutako International Airport for N$150,00. This service is a shared service with other passengers either on a sedan or a minibus. Departures are arranged for 2 hours before every departure flights and at least an hour after arrival flights. Take note that due to some delays on arrival flights as well as clearing through immigration, luggage collection and customs, there will be some delays. We will ensure departures on the provided times but collection on another schedule is possible. Your patience is highly appreciated. Pickup and drop-off can be arranged from your residence at additional cost to connect on this service. Out-Of-Schedule option is also available at extra cost should you not wish to wait for a certain hour.

  • Windhoek to Gaborone and Returns

    We have a weekly departure from Windhoek to Gaborone on Tuesdays 05h00 & Thursdays 14h00 and return Gaborone to Windhoek on Wednesdays & Fridays in the mornings at 05h00. Confirmation need to be done latest by the day before departure at 17h00. No reservations of seat accepted without payment.

Exclusive transfer for either airport or to any destination around Namibia and SADC countries are possible with either our sedan or minibuses. Clients decide on departure dates and times and the stops that need to be made as well as the routes.

Group events for schools, church, companies, funerals or any can also be arranged for around Windhoek, Namibia and SADC countries. Advance notice is required as well as a clear plan of what transport requirements are needed for the event.
